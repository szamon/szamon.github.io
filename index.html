Front End? Why?

After couple of adventureous years as an analyst I came to the conclusion, that I have had enough of Excel. But taking things seriously - during my work I used VBA and SQL a lot. This was when I first discovered that coding is "it".

From then on, in my free time, I was learning Front End from scratch by going through Front Dev Course on teamtreehouse and by gaining certficates on freecodecamp. So far, I have completed three certificates: RWD, JS Algorithms and Data Sctructures and Front End Libraries.

I realized that programming and creating websites are pleasure to me and I became passionate about JS. These are the reasons for which I am looking for position of Junior Front End Developer. I offer full commitment in exchange for the possibility to learn new and expand my current dev skills.
